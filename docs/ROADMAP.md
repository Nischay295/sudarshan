# Sudarshan Roadmap

## Phase 1: Accounting MVP

- Company setup with GST details.
- Indian chart-of-accounts template.
- Manual transaction entry and document placeholder.
- Automatic posting after deterministic validation.
- Trial balance, P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, and GST reports.
- Audit logs and correction-ready ledger model.

## Phase 2: Ingestion Automation

- OCR pipeline for invoices and receipts.
- Bank statement import.
- Duplicate invoice detection.
- Exception review queue.
- Account suggestion learning from prior approvals.

## Phase 3: Professional Intelligence

- CFO dashboard.
- Anomaly detection.
- Budgeting and forecasting.
- Management commentary and board-style summaries.
- Export packages for accountants and auditors.

## Phase 4: Commerce Knowledge Layer

- Research report workspace.
- CA/CFA/MBA learning mode.
- Viva and case-study practice.
- Research methodology assistant.
- Publication ethics and citation support.

## Phase 5: Enterprise Platform

- Multi-branch, multi-currency, and consolidation.
- ERP and banking integrations.
- Role-based approvals.
- SOC 2/ISO-aligned controls.
- Marketplace and API monetization.
